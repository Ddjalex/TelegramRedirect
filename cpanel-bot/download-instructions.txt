║           HOW TO DOWNLOAD YOUR BOT FILES                       ║
╚════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

All your PHP bot files are in the 'cpanel-bot' folder.

To download them to your computer:

METHOD 1: Using Replit Interface
---------------------------------
1. Open the Files panel on the left
2. Navigate to 'cpanel-bot' folder  
3. Right-click on each file
4. Select "Download"
5. Save to your computer


METHOD 2: Download All at Once
-------------------------------
1. Click the three dots (⋮) menu in Replit
2. Select "Download as zip"
3. Extract the 'cpanel-bot' folder from the zip


FILES YOU NEED TO UPLOAD TO CPANEL:
------------------------------------
✓ config.php
✓ webhook.php
✓ functions.php
✓ setup.php
✓ .htaccess
✓ README.md (optional - for reference)
✓ QUICK_START.txt (optional - for reference)


NEXT STEPS:
-----------
1. Download all files
2. Follow instructions in QUICK_START.txt
3. Upload to your cPanel
4. Configure and enjoy!
